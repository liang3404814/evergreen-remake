Who We Are

EGC is a Japanese men's choral group which got its start in January 2006. The first EGC concert was held on January 23rd at the official residence of the Consulate General of Japan in Seattle. Since then, EGC has performed in numerous venues around the Puget Sound area. Principally, musical selections are either noslagic and traditional Japanese songs but more recently have incorporated contemporary songs into its performance repertoire. Members practice weekly, ususlly on Sundays for two hours. We are constantly recruiting and welcoming new members
